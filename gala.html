The Gala

The St George Gala for Scholarship Funding

Plan to attend the next "The Gala" held in early November. As one of the few formal opportunities in St. George, it is the only source of funding for the scholarships awarded in May. There are silent and live auctions, and keynote speakers like Pat Jones, CEO of the Women's Leadership Institute. We recommend you get your tickets early as last year's event was sold out.

We'd like to acknowledge the sponsors for 2016 and invite you to be part of it as a sponsor and guest. Special thanks to JK Jewelers, Red Mountain Spa, Redstone Olive Oil Company, The Spectrum and the Travel Connection for their generous sponsorship.

Keep your eye out for the 2017 date and mark your calendar for a memorable event!
